Fire Detection

Instant smoke detection and coordinate notification

Pinpointing When a Forest Fire Starts with Artificial Intelligence

Wildfires pose a significant threat to both humans and wildlife. They cause great loss of life and property by destroying large rural and wild areas. That's why the ability to detect the occurrence of a wildfire is extremely important. Unfortunately, today many forest fire detection systems are based on human observation. This causes a delay in notifying the relevant parties. Although governments allocate more resources to firefighting, the average forest fire size is increasing for these reasons.

Uptecra Forest Fire Detection Solution instantly detects the fire point with artificial intelligence and 24/7 visual and infrared analysis and instantly transmits the relevant point information to first responders.
